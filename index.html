HI ALL, I'M JUSTIN 👋

With a background as a Technical Artist and currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, I focus on gameplay programming and interactive systems development. My strong creative instincts, paired with my technical skills, allow me to design immersive and intelligent experiences.

I enjoy blending logic with aesthetics. I'm proficient in Photoshop and love crafting detailed visual assets, especially during prototyping and early design phases.

My Master’s thesis explores how AI, particularly Large Language Models (LLMs), can enhance interactivity and narrative depth in role-playing games. I’ve also applied AI in healthcare through a health informatics course, broadening my perspective on its cross-industry impact.
